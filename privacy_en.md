## II. Collection and Use of Personal Information
### (I) Account Login Information
This App only supports Apple ID authorization login. During the login process, we do not actively collect your Apple ID account, password, or other core information.

According to Apple's official authorization mechanism, we may obtain non-sensitive personal information authorized by you (including only user nickname and user avatar), which is only used for:
1.  Completing account registration and login verification for this App;
2.  Displaying your personal account information to help you identify your login status.

### (II) Device and Usage Information
To ensure the normal operation of this App, optimize service experience, and troubleshoot issues, we will automatically collect the following necessary device and usage information during your use of this App:
1.  Basic device information: device model, operating system version, device unique identifier (only for device identification, not associated with personal identity information);
2.  Service usage information: App function click records, service operation logs (only for troubleshooting technical issues such as App crashes and freezes, not involving personal privacy content).

### (III) Information We Do Not Collect
Except for the information explicitly listed above, we do not collect any other personal information from you, including but not limited to: contacts, location, call records, SMS content, photo albums, bank account information, etc.

## III. Storage and Protection of Personal Information
1.  **Storage Period:** We only store your personal information for the shortest necessary period to achieve the service purposes described in this policy. After you cancel your account or stop using this App's services, we will delete your relevant personal information within a reasonable period, except as otherwise required by laws and regulations.
2.  **Storage Location:** Your personal information is stored only on secure servers, with support for data synchronization through iCloud (requires your active enablement). iCloud data is stored in your personal iCloud account, which we cannot access.
3.  **Security Protection:** We adopt security protection measures that meet industry standards (including data encryption, access control, firewalls, etc.) to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, damage, or loss of your personal information. However, please note that no network transmission method or storage technology can guarantee 100% security, and we will make every effort to protect your personal information security.

## IV. Sharing and Disclosure of Personal Information
We will not sell, rent, or transfer any of your personal information to any third party, and may only share or disclose your personal information in the following limited circumstances:
1.  With your explicit written authorization or consent;
2.  To comply with mandatory requirements of laws, regulations, administrative rules, judicial judgments, or rulings;
3.  To maintain public interest and protect the legitimate rights and interests of us or other users (such as preventing fraud, handling security incidents, etc.), within a reasonable and necessary scope of disclosure.
